About Me

Maicol Osorio

Founder of Maicol Photography

I photograph people so they can be seen as who they are today and own their presence in the frame. Most people pull back when the camera comes out, so I work with you until the way you want to be seen comes through on camera and still feels like you.

I specialize in headshots, portraits, and branding for individuals, teams, and companies who want images that elevate their brand and presence.

If you take your work seriously, your images should reflect that.
